Registration Key Recovery

The registration key is sent to you by email automatically after the transaction has been completed. To recover a lost registration key, enter your email address below to look it up.

Open the received registration key with Cyberduck by double clicking. Make sure the filename of the registration key attached ends with .cyberducklicense. Refer to the documentation if you have trouble receiving or opening the key.
