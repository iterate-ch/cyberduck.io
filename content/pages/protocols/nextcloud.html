Features

Alternative to Nextcloud ior ownCloud client. Selectively sync folders and files using Mountain Duck instead of keeping a full copy of all files on your computer.

Support for specific features available in Nextcloud & owncloud.

Modification Date
Sets the X-OC-Mtime header to keep the modification date of files uploaded.

Share Files
Create download and upload links to share files with others.
