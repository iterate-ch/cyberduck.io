Google Drive Access your Google Drive without syncing to your computer.

Documentation

Features

Selectively sync folders and files using Mountain Duck and access files when offline.

Team Drive
Use the regular Google Drive connection type to see the Team Drives besides My Drive and Shared with me.

Shared files
Documents shared with you from another Google account can be found inside the top level folder Shared with me.

Documents from Google Docs
Opens documents from Google Docs in your web browser for editing.
